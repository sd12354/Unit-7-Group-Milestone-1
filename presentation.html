The Problem

Study coordination is broken

Students rely on scattered group chats, DMs, and hallway conversations to organise study sessions — most plans never materialise into an actual meeting.

📱

Plans lost in group chats that never get actioned

🤷

No easy way to find open study sessions near you

⏰

No reminders — people forget and sessions fall apart

The Solution

StudySync turns plans into sessions

🔐

Secure Accounts

Email/password auth backed by Firebase — your identity travels with you.

📋

Post a Session

Title, subject, time, location, and a cap on attendees — in under a minute.

🔍

Browse & Filter

Live feed of upcoming sessions. Filter by subject or location keyword.

✅

One-Tap Join

Transactional join respects capacity limits. Leave just as easily.

🗺️

Open in Maps

Tap the location to jump straight to Apple Maps — no copy-paste needed.

👤

Profiles

Display name, bio, and a clean view of sessions you're hosting or joined.

Live Demo

A day in the life of a StudySync user

1

Sign Up & Build a Profile

Maya registers with her email, sets a display name, and adds a short bio so study partners know who she is.

2

Browse the Feed

She opens the Home tab and sees upcoming CS 101 and Calculus sessions. She filters by "CS" to narrow things down.

3

Join a Session

Maya taps a "CS 101 Finals Prep" card, reads the details, sees 3/5 spots taken, and hits Join. Done.

4

Host Her Own

She creates a Calculus study block for tomorrow at the library. Her friends see it in the feed and join.
